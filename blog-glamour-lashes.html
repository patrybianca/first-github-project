    Eyelash Extensions? All the information you need to know about it!

    • By Glamour
    • 2022

    Are you curious to know everything about eyelash extensions, a practice that is becoming more and more common? We will see together all the application methods present at the moment, we will talk about their duration, how to prepare before the treatment and how to take care of them later!

    It is a technique that has become popular mainly because it allows you to look made up, even when you wake up, making mascara a practically useless product! If you want to find out everything there is to know about the world of eyelash extensions, then read on!

    The application of eyelash extensions allows you to visually correct the eye. For example, if you have close-set eyes, you just need to apply long lashes, especially on the outside of the eye - and vice versa. A true dream!

    At the moment we can divide the application of eyelash extensions into three broad categories: one-to-one technique, 2D / 3D / 4D / 5D / 6D volume technique and the most popular because it is fast and with an open eye, SPRINT. What is the most effective technique? There is no real answer to this question. It all depends on your lashes and the result you want to achieve.

    Glamour Lashes

    Glamour Lashes is an Italian line that guarantees high quality professional products for the eyelash sector.

    Lashes

    Our lashes are produced specifically for use as an eyelash extension and are very light and bright, they are all hand made with synthetic materials, also our quality control tests that the lashes are all with the right curvature and length.

    Texture

    Our lashes are soft, light and flexible, which gives them a very natural look like real lashes.

    Performance

    The fibers of our lashes are covered with a special formula so that they adhere better between natural and synthetic lashes. Furthermore, in their surface they have micro pores that allow the glue to adhere better. Our lashes are available in various thicknesses, lengths, curves and colors.

    B-CURL / C-CURL / D-CURL / L-CURL


    Curvature "B": Medium-curved lashes ideal for clients who have naturally straight lashes.

    Curvature "C": They have a very pronounced curvature and give a fantastic curved effect.

    Curvature "D": They have an extreme curvature, they are used when you want a strong and suggestive opening of the gaze.

    "L" Curvature: Ideal for Asian eyes, for eyes with drooping lids or for those who wear glasses.
